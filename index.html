Charming Gathering

Dear esteemed guests,

Family Scott is most delighted to extend to you an invitation to a series of enchanting gatherings towards the end of this month. The schedule is as follows:

Monday, the 24th: We are set to receive the esteemed Gill and family. Their arrival by carriage is expected in the post meridiem. Evening shall be dedicated to cordial greetings and congenial drinks.

Tuesday, the 25th: The weather appears to be in our favor, suggesting an excursion to Harwich and a visit to the beach.

Wednesday, the 26th: We shall once again find ourselves at Harwich beach during the day. In the post meridiem, we anticipate the arrival of the gracious Anne. The evening shall be filled with warm greetings and a supper featuring exquisite fish.

Thursday, the 27th: With a forecast of rain, we are considering a visit to the museums of Harwich or perhaps another rain-proof activity. Geoff is expected to join us mid-afternoon via train. The afternoon, despite its overcast appearance, promises to be quite pleasing for a local excursion.

Friday, the 28th: The day is expected to be rainy, suggesting an expedition to Walton Pier for some delightful light entertainment. Alternatively, we could venture to Colchester or partake in other indoor activities. We eagerly anticipate Sally's arrival by airship in the evening.

Saturday, the 29th: The weather forecast predicts sunshine, a miraculous occurrence indeed! This will be an opportune day for the beach. Barbeque and games at Hamford are planned for the afternoon and early evening.

Sunday, the 30th: We shall partake in a traditional Sunday lunch at a suitable location and then spend time in each other's company. As the day progresses, guests may choose to depart at their convenience.

We look forward to your presence in these gatherings. Your participation will indeed make these events memorable.

Yours sincerely,

Family Scott
